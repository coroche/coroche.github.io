A project inspired by Irish Peaks.

Keeping track of my progress through all peaks in Ireland over a height of 600m with a prominance greater than 15m. Also included is the "Highest Hundred" (a sublist of the Vandeleur-Lynams). These are the highest 100 peaks in Ireland with a prominance greater than 100m.
